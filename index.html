Our History

The Department of Energy has one of the richest
and most diverse histories in the Federal Government.
Although only in existence since 1977, the Department
traces its lineage to the Manhattan Project effort to develop
the atomic bomb during World War II, and to the various energy-related
programs that previously had been dispersed throughout various Federal agencies
